BMW G310R

BMW G310R

Price: ₹15000/day

Overview: The BMW G 310 R is an entry-level standard motorcycle. It is BMW's first, modern, low-end, beginner motorcycle sold under the BMW Motorrad brand. It debuted in November 2015 with global sales beginning in 2018.BMW released the G 310 GS, an adventure bike based on the G 310 R, on July 19, 2018.

The bike is powered by a four-valve 313 cc (19.1 cu in) liquid-cooled single-cylinder engine shared with the TVS Apache RR 310 that BMW says produces 25 kW (33.6 bhp) power at 9,500 rpm and 28 N⋅m (21 lbf⋅ft) torque at 7,500 rpm. The engine configuration has the intake in front of the engine, and the exhaust behind it.

It has self start, static rev limiter, counterbalance shaft, wet sump, and electronic fuel injection and requires at least 95 RON petrol. It is compatible with up to 10% ethanol (E10). The fuel capacity is 11 L (2.4 imp gal; 2.9 US gal). The instrument panel consist of a monochrome segment LCD display with engine temperature meter. There are two auxiliary power sockets and a steering lock.

Features: The G 310 R has a length of 1988 mm, width of 896 mm and height of 1227 mm, a wheelbase of 1374 mm, and a seat height of 785 mm. The steering angle is 64.9°. It has an upside-down fork and a mono rear shock absorber with adjustable preload. The bike has a front 110/70 R17 and a rear 150/60 R17 Michelin Pilot Street Radial tubeless tyre and 5-spoke alloy rims.

The braking of the bike features a dual-channel anti-lock braking system, with the front using a single 300 mm disc, radially-bolted 4-piston fixed-caliper brake and the rear, a single 240 mm disc, single-piston floating caliper brake. It has an axial front master brake cylinder. The brakes are made by ByBre.

The BMW G 310 R has a fuel tank capacity of 11 liters, providing a decent range for both city and highway riding. Its fuel economy is efficient, offering approximately 30-35 km/l depending on the riding style. For safety and performance, it comes with standard features such as a slipper clutch, which reduces engine braking effects and improves smoothness during aggressive downshifts. Additionally, the bike is built with high-quality materials, ensuring durability and attention to detail.
