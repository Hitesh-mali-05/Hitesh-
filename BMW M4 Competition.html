BMW M4 Competition

BMW M4 Competition

Price: ₹500/day

Overview:The BMW M4 Competition is a high-performance sports coupe that blends cutting-edge technology, dynamic handling, and bold design. It’s part of BMW's M Series and serves as a more powerful, track-focused version of the regular BMW 4 Series.

The M4 Competition boasts a muscular and sporty design with a wide stance, large air intakes, and an aggressive front grille that emphasizes its performance heritage.

Minimizes body roll during sharp turns or high-speed cornering, ensuring better stability and agility. A mechanical rear limited-slip differential optimizes rear-wheel power distribution, enhancing traction during spirited driving or track use

Enhanced for better stability and road presence. Includes adjustable rear spoilers and active front flaps to optimize airflow and downforce.

The M4 features Active Sound, live amplification of the engine's natural sound inducted into the passenger cabin via speakers in the car. BMW claims this technology has been used so that the well insulated cabin can reduce road/wind noise but still provide the driver with the sporty sound of the M powered engine. There are no artificial sound or any pre-recorded track in the system. This system was first implemented in the M5

Features: It has an aggressive front grille, wide wheel arches, and aerodynamic features like an adjustable rear spoiler, making it stand out as a performance-oriented vehicle.

The M4 Competition comes with an 8-speed M Steptronic automatic transmission, and xDrive (all-wheel drive) is available for enhanced traction.

It is powered by a 3.0L twin-turbocharged inline-six engine producing 503 horsepower and 479 lb-ft of torque, allowing it to accelerate from 0-60 mph in around 3.8 seconds.
