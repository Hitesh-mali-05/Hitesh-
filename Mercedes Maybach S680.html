Mercedes Maybach S680

Mercedes Maybach S680

Price: ₹7700/day

Overview:The Mercedes-Maybach line represents the pinnacle of luxury, performance, and technology within the Mercedes-Benz brand. Maybach, originally a standalone luxury car manufacturer, became a sub-brand of Mercedes-Benz in 2002.

The interiors of Maybach cars are adorned with the finest materials. Expect hand-finished leathers, real wood veneers, and polished metals. The stitching is meticulous, and the overall interior design is all about comfort, elegance, and attention to detail.

Maybach cars are equipped with air suspension systems that adjust the ride height and damping characteristics to provide the most comfortable and smooth ride. This, combined with advanced soundproofing and insulation, ensures that the ride is as serene as possible.

A large panoramic sunroof allows natural light into the cabin, enhancing the feeling of space and comfort. The 360-degree camera system provides a clear view of the car’s surroundings, aiding in parking and maneuvering in tight spaces.

The car features a two-tone paint job consisting of Nautical Blue and Rose Gold above and below the belt line, respectively. This color is exclusive to the Haute Voiture. It sports monoblock wheels painted to match the blue paint on the upper body. The body is also adorned with chrome trim.

Features: Mercedes-Maybach vehicles are powered by some of the most potent engines available in the Mercedes lineup. This includes V8 and V12 engines, which provide a smooth yet powerful driving experience. In particular, the V12 engine offers a quiet, refined, and effortless drive, emphasizing comfort over outright performance.

The Maybach S-Class and Maybach GLS typically feature powerful V8 and V12 engines. The V8 produces up to 496 hp, while the V12 engine (available in some variants) offers a commanding 621 hp and 738 lb-ft of torque.

The Maybach S-Class can accelerate from 0-60 mph in just 4.1 seconds with the V12 engine, despite its size and weight, showcasing an excellent balance of power and comfort.The Maybach GLS SUV comes standard with 4MATIC all-wheel drive, ensuring stability and superior handling, especially in challenging driving conditions.
