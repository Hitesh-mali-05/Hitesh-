Overview:The Rolls-Royce Ghost is a full-sized luxury car manufactured by Rolls-Royce Motor Cars. During development, the Ghost was known as the "RR04". It was designed as a smaller, "more measured, more realistic car" than the Phantom. The Ghost features exceptional sound insulation, with a design focused on creating a whisper-quiet cabin. Triple-layered windows, soundproof doors, and thick carpets all work together to block out the outside world.

The Ghost has a 129.7-inch (3,290 mm) wheelbase, roof height, bonnet height and track widths all of its own, and the Ghost uses Phantom-style air springs. The car also shares the FlexRay electronic system with its larger stablemate.

The iconic Rolls-Royce grille is large and imposing, framed by a sleek, angular design. The grille is often lit by LED lights, creating a visually striking effect at night. This signature design feature has been refined to create a flowing line that gives the car a balanced, elegant presence.

The Rolls-Royce Ghost Black badge is a high performance variant of the Rolls-Royce Ghost Second Generation. It was unveiled in October 2021. It differs from the standard model by appearance due to black colour detailing.
