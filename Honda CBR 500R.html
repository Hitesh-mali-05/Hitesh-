Honda CBR 500R

Honda CBR 500R

Price: ₹1700/day

Overview: The Honda CBR 500R is a popular middleweight sportbike known for its perfect balance of performance, style, and practicality. Positioned in the 500cc class, it offers an excellent entry point for riders looking to transition from beginner bikes to something more performance-oriented, while still being manageable for newer riders.

A large Thin-Film Transistor (TFT) instrument display is bright and easy to read and features a digital speedometer, tachometer, odometer, and more.The diamond-shaped steel-tube mainframe is seamlessly connected to the engine through four mounts and provides a rock-solid foundation for an unrivaled ride and handling experience.

The CBR 500R carries the iconic supersport styling of Honda’s larger CBR models, with sharp lines, aggressive bodywork, and a streamlined fairing. Its aerodynamic design helps enhance performance at higher speeds, while maintaining comfort for everyday riding. The stepped two-piece seat offers both comfort and sportiness, and the bike is available in vibrant color schemes, including Racing Red, Matte Gunpowder Black, and Pearl White.

One of the key highlights of the CBR 500R is its impressive fuel efficiency, with the bike capable of covering long distances on a single tank. This makes it a great option for daily commuters, touring, and even weekend rides.

Features: The motorcycle is powered by a 471cc liquid-cooled parallel-twin engine, featuring Programmed Fuel Injection (PGM-FI) and a slipper clutch. It boasts a supersport styling and is equipped with a stepped two-piece seat. A two-piece seat with a dual-level design accommodates various riders and passengers, making it perfect for urban streets and winding roads.

The CBR 500R is powered by a 471cc liquid-cooled parallel-twin engine that produces around 47 horsepower, making it a great mix of power and efficiency. The engine is tuned for a smooth, linear power delivery, offering excellent throttle response, and providing a thrilling yet manageable ride for both newer and experienced riders. The engine is paired with a 6-speed transmission and features Programmed Fuel Injection (PGM-FI), ensuring smooth and efficient power delivery.

The Honda CBR 500R features a slipper clutch that helps prevent rear-wheel hopping during aggressive downshifting, ensuring smoother performance. It is equipped with sharp LED lighting, including a headlamp and tail lamp, for improved visibility. The bike also comes with a full LCD instrument display, which includes a speedometer, tachometer, fuel gauge, and trip meter in a sleek, modern format.
